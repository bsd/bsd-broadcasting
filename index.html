#BackTheBBC

The BBC belongs to all of us.

Join the grassroots campaign fighting to protect it!

In 2016, our beloved national broadcasting service faces it’s biggest threat yet.

It’s now clear that the government is looking to use the forthcoming charter renewal to diminish the BBC, to reduce it to another competitor fighting for profit in a market of cheap programming and biased journalism.

The BBC is an invaluable institution, it shines a mirror on who we are as a nation — and most importantly it belongs to us, and it answers to us, the public. We’re in a position of unique privilege to be able to hold our national broadcaster to account, but we are also responsible for protecting it — and right now, it needs our help more than ever.

We don’t know the scale or shape of the threat that’s coming, but we know we’ll be a more powerful ally when we stand together. So can we count on you to back the BBC in 2016?
